About Gabe

 Posted on March 30, 2026  |  Gabe Stein

Gabe is the founder of Studio Boshi and the Gabe Stein Youtube Channel, where he teaches game programming concepts and related things. Most of his games are made without engines and he likes to code at a low level using languages like C. He’s also been a big proponent of Lua.

He lived in Kawasaki, Japan for 9 years before moving back to his hometown of Toronto, Canada. He’s currently developing Ocean City Killer, a retro cyberpunk adventure. He enjoys cycling, photography, and model making.
